Mixed Feelings: Back2school

9.21.15

Contributed by Renan Machado,
Departmental DJ

Welp! It’s about that time again boys, girls, and other genders. Foundation student or first grader, those first day feels are back again. Time to scope out who got hot over the summer and hug all your old friends. Also time for some kickass back to school parties.

But let us not forget the usual load of stress and existential angst that comes with another year of art school. So here’s where this playlist comes in. Fair warning, I loaded this baby with some attitude. We got Van Halen and Dead Prez on the same playlist so be warned this is not for the faint of heart. Overall I kept things optimistic though, because ya really won’t do so hot if you think everything sucks. Plug your earphones in, crank up your macbook speakers, and remember the great and almighty advice of dead prez “my word of advice to you is just relax, Just do what you got to do, if that don’t work then kick the facts.”


Be positive, and be strong. I believe in you and so do the B-52s.
